WHERE TO START?

You want a commission but don't know where to start? here is a simple guide on how to commission me.

1-Inspiration

Gather as much references as you can for your idea, think about the characters, their outfits, the colors or the brackground you would like. The more information the easier it will be for me to capture the feel of the illustration that you want.

2-Be Patient

good art takes time and care, I will update you thought the illustration process so you can give me feedback on what you would like to change or what you like to keep, this piece is for you so don't be shy and let me know what you like!

3-Enjoy

Once the piece is finished you will get to enjoy something made especially for you or your loved one, print it, use it in your social media, or even get it on a shirt! This piece of art is yours to enjoy.
