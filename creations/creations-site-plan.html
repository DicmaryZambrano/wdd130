About

About:
Here I will create a small biography about me and my history with art so the client can know a bit more about me.

MEET DICMARY ZAMBRANO
(Explain how Dicmary Zambrano started creating art) Hi! My name is Dicmary Zambrano, I am a 20-year-old digital artist from
Venezuela that dedicates their life to learning and creating art. Ever since I was a little kid, I loved to learn new things.
Arts and crafts, painting and drawing even sewing. If it meant I could bring something new to the world be sure I was doing it!

(Show how Dicmary Zambrano's caught people's interest with her art) When I was in high school, I started to take drawing more seriously,
and people started to come to me asking if I could make them a drawing. Before I knew I started to be recognized as a talented artist around
school, this encouraged me to keep working on my skills and nowadays I am still teaching myself how to be even better than yesterday. I started
to do commission in the hopes I could keep funding my education while I create beautiful illustrations for people.

(Share Dicmary Zambrano's passion about her art career) There is just something very special in seeing people get exited when they see the
art I made for them, it is a satisfying experience and shading my art with people is what has helped me get this far. I am very grateful for
all the people that find value in what I do and I will do my best to keep creating for all of them.

Commissions

Commissions: In this page I will detail everything the audience needs to know before commissioning me.
WHERE TO START?
(Create a simple guide for people that are commissioning for the first time) You want a commission but don't know where to start? here
is a simple guide on how to commission me.

1 - Inspiration: Gather as much references as you can for your idea, think about the characters, their outfits, the colors or the background
you would like. The more information the easier it will be for me to capture the feel of the illustration that you want.

2- Contact me: Go to my contact page and use the provided contact information to send me a message detailing what you want, you can find all
my contact information here.

3- Be patient: good art takes time and care, I will update you thought the illustration process so you can give me feedback on what you would
like to change or what you like to keep, this piece is for you so don't be shy and let me know what you like!

4- Enjoy!: Once the piece is finished you will get to enjoy something made especially for you or your loved one, print it, use it in your social
media, or even get it on a shirt! This piece of art is yours to enjoy.
